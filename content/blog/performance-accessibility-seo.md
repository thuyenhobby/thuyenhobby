Website nhanh hon thuong cung de dung hon va de crawl hon. Khi thiet ke tu dau, hay bat dau
bang HTML semantic, typography de doc, image toi uu va navigation ro rang.

## Checklist ngan

- Dung heading theo thu tu.
- Kiem tra contrast trong light mode va dark mode.
- Viet title va description rieng cho tung route.
- Do luong truoc khi them animation nang.
